  8  """ 
  9  Physical quantities with units. 
 10   
 11  This module provides a data type that represents a physical 
 12  quantity together with its unit. It is possible to add and 
 13  subtract these quantities if the units are compatible, and 
 14  a quantity can be converted to another compatible unit. 
 15  Multiplication, subtraction, and raising to integer powers 
 16  is allowed without restriction, and the result will have 
 17  the correct unit. A quantity can be raised to a non-integer 
 18  power only if the result can be represented by integer powers 
 19  of the base units. 
 20   
 21  The values of physical constants are taken from the 1986 
 22  recommended values from CODATA. Other conversion factors 
 23  (e.g. for British units) come from various sources. I can't 
 24  guarantee for the correctness of all entries in the unit 
 25  table, so use this at your own risk.
